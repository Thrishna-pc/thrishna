Four Wheelers:

  2. Car
  3. Bus

Two Wheelers:

Functions:

Bus:
Buses are used for public transportation, school transport, and tourism.
Auto Rickshaw:
An auto rickshaw is a motorized, three-wheeled vehicle often used for short-distance public transportation in urban areas.
